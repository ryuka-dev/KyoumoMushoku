# Factory Background Fronts v01

Generated on 2026-07-16 with the built-in image generation tool, then locally processed for project import.

Purpose: distant background factory front sprites for a bleak low-saturation urban side-scroller scene.

Style constraints:
- True low-resolution pixel art after local downscaling.
- Front elevation only.
- Distant-background readability over foreground detail density.
- Muted gray, soot, dirty concrete, faded brick, rust, and dim window colors.
- No readable text, logos, people, vehicles, shadows, or ground plane.
- Source images were generated on a flat solid `#ff00ff` chroma-key background and converted to transparent PNGs.
- Final transparent sprites are bottom-aligned on fixed transparent canvases and quantized to 32 colors.
