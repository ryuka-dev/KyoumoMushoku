## Prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: AI raw character concept for a 2D side-scrolling Unity game
Primary request: Create the main protagonist character for the game 今日も無職。 as a strict pixel-art raw draft.
Subject: a tiny unemployed Japanese man, one-head-tall proportions. The head takes about 70% of the full body height, with a very short body and tiny legs. He has messy dark hair, exhausted half-closed eyes, a worn dark hoodie, faded pants, old sneakers, and holds a plain plastic grocery bag with no text. His posture is slouched and tired, but he is still standing and surviving.
Style/medium: strict low-resolution pixel art sprite concept, limited palette, crisp square pixels, no anti-aliasing, no painterly shading, no smooth gradients. Muted low-saturation urban colors. Readable silhouette at small size. Grounded everyday realism with dry black humor and social satire. Not cute mascot, not heroic, not anime idol.
Composition/framing: full-body side-view standing pose, facing right, centered on a perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background. Generous padding around the character. No shadow, no ground plane, no UI, no text, no watermark. This is a single character raw draft, not an illustration scene.
Lighting/mood: flat readable game-sprite lighting, urban exhaustion, mundane Japanese city survival mood, not horror.
Constraints: one-head-tall, squat silhouette, gameplay-scale sprite feeling, no readable text on the bag or clothing. Keep details simple enough for pixel art and later manual cleanup.
Avoid: tall body proportions, 2-head body, 3-head body, normal chibi, cute mascot, detailed anime face, painterly illustration, soft shading, anti-aliased edges, smooth vector art, 3D render, realistic painting, fake Japanese text, logo text, heroic pose, cyberpunk, fantasy, horror monster, zombie, cluttered background.
```
